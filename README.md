## ğŸ® How to Play

1. **Click the Boss**: Each click deals damage to the boss and earns you points.
2. **Watch your Energy**: Clicking costs energy. If you run out, wait for it to regenerate or buy recovery upgrades.
3. **Upgrade Yourself**: Use your hard-earned points in the upgrades panel:
   - **Strength**: Deal more damage per click.
   - **Recovery**: Regenerate energy faster.
   - **Max HP**: Increase your survivability.
   - **Max Energy**: Carry more energy for longer burst attacks.
4. **Defeat all 10 Levels**: Can you beat the final boss?
